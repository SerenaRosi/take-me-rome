This apartment is less than 1 km from the Vatican and a 16-minute walk from Piazza Navona.

Location

Our apartment is located in Prati, a lively and historical neighborhood in Rome, known for its elegant streets, charming boutiques, and bustling atmosphere. Located near the Vatican City, it offers a mix of historical landmarks and modern amenities. Visitors can enjoy delightful cafes, restaurants serving authentic Italian cuisine, and a vibrant shopping scene. Prati's strategic location and excellent public transport connections make it a popular choice for both locals and tourists looking to explore the wonders of the Eternal City.

Popular points of interest near the apartment include:

  • Musei Vaticani - Vatican Museums
  • Piazza San Pietro - St. Peter's Square
  • Piazza Del Popolo - People's Square

Transports:
