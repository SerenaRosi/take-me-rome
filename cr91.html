Divine Vatican View Apartment

× ❮ ❯

In a central area of Rome, set within a short distance of Castel Sant'Angelo and Vatican City,
we propose our Divine Vatican View Apartment!

The spacious 3-bedroom apartment includes 2 bathrooms, bed linen, towels and a flat-screen cable TV. It also includes a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen and a balcony overlooking the inner courtyard. The apartment is non-smoking and soundproofed. The apartment can accommodate up to 10 people as in the first room we have 2 double beds, in the second 2 single beds and in the third 2 double beds. Located in the centre of Rome, in the historical Prati district, a short distance from Castel Sant'Angelo and Ottaviano Metro Station, Take me Rome - Divine Vatican View Apartment offers free WiFi, air conditioning, a dishwasher and a coffee machine. Guests have access to a lift and a hot tub.

Location

Our apartment is located in Prati, a lively and historical neighborhood in Rome, known for its elegant streets, charming boutiques, and bustling atmosphere. Located near the Vatican City, it offers a mix of historical landmarks and modern amenities. Visitors can enjoy delightful cafes, restaurants serving authentic Italian cuisine, and a vibrant shopping scene. Prati's strategic location and excellent public transport connections make it a popular choice for both locals and tourists looking to explore the wonders of the Eternal City.

Popular points of interest nearby include:
  • Musei Vaticani - Vatican Museums
  • Piazza San Pietro - St. Peter's Square
  • Piazza Del Popolo - People's Square
Transports:
