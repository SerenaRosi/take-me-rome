Location

The Aurelio district is a residential area located in Rome, Italy. Situated in the western part of Rome, it's known for its quiet and residential character, making it ideal for families and those seeking a more relaxed environment compared to the bustling city center. One of the district's notable features is its numerous green spaces and parks, including the Villa Doria Pamphili Park, one of Rome's largest parks, providing opportunities for outdoor activities and relaxation. Aurelio offers a variety of services, including shops, restaurants, schools, and recreational facilities. It is well-connected by public transportation, making it easily accessible from other parts of the city. Additionally, its proximity to the Vatican City adds a touch of historical and cultural significance to the area.
