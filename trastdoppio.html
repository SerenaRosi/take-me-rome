Location

Trastevere, a captivating neighborhood in Rome, lies on the Tiber's western bank. With cobblestone streets, medieval architecture, and Renaissance charm, it's a picturesque haven. Wander through its alleys to find colorful buildings, cozy piazzas, and an array of trattorias. At night, the area comes alive with bars and eateries serving Roman delicacies. Trastevere maintains its authentic vibe, boasting artisan shops and the historic Basilica of Santa Maria. This neighborhood invites you to uncover its hidden treasures and experience the heart of Rome's artistic and cultural allure.

Popular points of interest nearby include:
  • Basilica di S. Maria in Trastevere
  • Gianicolo
  • Isola Tiberina
  • Piazza San Cosimato
  • Piazza Navona
  • Piazza Venezia
