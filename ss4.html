Colosseo Terrace Apartment

Just in front of the most iconic Roman piece of history, we propose our Colosseo Terrace Apartment.

× ❮ ❯

Inside the wonderful Colosseum Terrace Apartment you will find 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, bed linen and towels, a flat-screen cable TV, a dining area, a fully equipped kitchen and a terrace with city views. The apartment can accommodate up to 6 people, as the three bedrooms are all equipped with a comfortable double bed. Located in the centre of Rome, Take Me Rome - Colosseo Terrace Apartment is 200 metres from Domus Aurea and 200 metres from Coliseum. Piazza Venezia is 1.3 km from the apartment.

Location

The neighborhood where the property is located is a fascinating area that is full of history. Located near the Colosseum, you'll find yourself immersed in the ancient heart of Rome. Walking through the streets of the neighborhood, you can admire the historic architecture, picturesque squares and charming alleys. Nearby you'll find a wide selection of restaurants, cafes, and shops, where you can savor authentic Italian cuisine, sip espresso, or shop trendy. You'll be steps away from major tourist attractions such as the Colosseum, Roman Forum and Circus Maximus, which will allow you to fully immerse yourself in the history and culture of Rome. The neighborhood is also well connected with public transit, allowing you to easily explore other areas of the city. You'll have access to nearby buses, trams, and subway stations, which will allow you to comfortably reach all the main attractions of Rome. Whether you're interested in history, art, cooking, or shopping, the neighborhood offers a variety of unique experiences that will make your stay in Rome unforgettable. I invite you to explore and discover all that this charming area has to offer during your stay.
